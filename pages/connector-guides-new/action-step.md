## Cyclr Setup

*   Go to your **Cyclr Console**.
*   Select the **Connectors** menu.
*   Select **Connector Library**.
*   Find the **Action Step** Connector.
*   Select the **Setup** button.

Enter the required values:
* **Client ID**
* **Client Secret**
